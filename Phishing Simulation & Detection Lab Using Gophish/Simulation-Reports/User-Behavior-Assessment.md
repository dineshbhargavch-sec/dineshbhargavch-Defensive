# User Behavior & Awareness Assessment

## Objective
Analyze user behavior patterns during a phishing simulation to identify awareness gaps.

## Behavioral Indicators Observed
- Clicking links without hovering to inspect URLs.
- Trusting internal-looking email templates.
- Failure to verify with IT before submitting credentials.

## Positive Behaviors
- Users who reported phishing avoided credential submission.
- Awareness-trained users identified red flags early.

## Common Mistakes
- Ignoring sender domain mismatches.
- Acting quickly due to fear of account suspension.

## Training Recommendations
- Simulated phishing every quarter.
- Visual training on how to inspect URLs.
- Clear internal reporting mechanisms for suspicious emails.
